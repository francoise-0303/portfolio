Te Māramataka: Relational to Your Land

The Māramataka follows the tohu, the signs. It lives in relationship with the land beneath your feet and the sky above you.

Aroha's Atelier · Indigenous Reconnection

The Māramataka is relational. It lives in the relationship between observer and sky, earth and season. The lunar nights cycle (Whiro through Tireo) remains the same everywhere. What changes is the season, the stars visible, the activities of the land, and the way each place responds.
