Indigenous Reconnection

Learning to read the sky, the land, and the seasons again

The Māramataka is the Māori lunar calendar. It connects the phases of the moon and the rising of stars to the planting cycles, fishing seasons, and daily rhythms of life in Aotearoa. These pages are a starting point for reconnection: with the sky above you and the land beneath your feet.
